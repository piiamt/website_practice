About me

Piia Maria Tomberg is a person with a diverse background. She is an Estonian artist, a member of the LGBTQ+ community and a PhD student in astrophysics. Her fascination for the natural world of this beautiful planet is apparent in the endless inspiration she draws from the night sky and all that it can contain.

Piia also finds joy in sharing her creations. She often shares videos of her painting process in satisfying videos. Piia most enjoys the medium of oil painting, as it best provides the freedom of creation that brings so much joy from painting.

If you'd like to contact me then please feel free to send me an email at piiamt.tinuviel@gmail.com
