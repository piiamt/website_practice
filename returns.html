Refund policy

If you have any issues with your order I would be happy to help and make things right.

We have a 30-day return policy, which means you have 30 days after receiving your item to contact us.

To be eligible for return, your item must be in the same condition that you received it, unused and in its original packaging, also the receipt we send as proof of purchase.

To start the return process you can contact us at piiamt.tinuviel@gmail.com. If your return is accepted, we will send you your return shipping label along with instructions for sending the package. Items sent back without first requesting a return will not be accepted.

If you have any questions about our return policy, feel free to contact us at piiamt.tinuviel@gmail.com.

If your item is damaged or defective, please contact us so that we can fix the issue.

We will notify you as soon as we have received your return and will let you know if the refund is approved. If approved, we will refund you via PayPal.
